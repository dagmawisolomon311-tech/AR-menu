OUR STORY

A house of two fires.

In Dukem, we grew up between the aroma of freshly roasted coffee and the hiss of a hot grill. Tinsae opened this house to serve both — the raw, sacred cuts of kurt and ketfo prepared the way our grandmothers taught, alongside the burgers and pizzas that the new generation of Dukem craves.

Every plate begins the same way: fire, salt, spice, patience. What follows is up to your appetite.
